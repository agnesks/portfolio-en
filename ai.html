Project Overview

This project focuses on redesigning an existing learning module into a more structured, learner-centered experience with clear and measurable learning outcomes. The project was based on an existing script and previously established learning outcomes, which I restructured and redesigned into an interactive Articulate Rise module.

The original material contained unclear learning outcomes and activities that focused on recognition rather than application. Some tasks were not aligned with the intended learning goals, and measurability was missing.

The main focus of this project was to move from passive content consumption to active decision-making, while ensuring that learning outcomes are achieved.

I reworked the content structure for better flow. Replaced classification-based tasks with conceptual learning and added scenario-based exercises to support decision-making and application.

⚠️ Problems:

  • Learners don’t understand how AI can streamline their everyday workflows
  • The existing training is too theoretical and not applicable
  • Learners can recall concepts but can’t apply them

🎯 Goal:

  • Improve understanding of AI in workplace contexts
  • Help employees make better data-driven decisions
  • Increase efficiency through AI-supported workflows

📈 Learning Outcomes:

By the end of the module, learners will be able to:
  • Identify how AI can support decision-making in different workplace scenarios
  • Recognize opportunities to use AI for improving efficiency and workflows
  • Evaluate how AI can enhance customer service processes (e.g. chatbots, personalization, complaint handling)
  • Apply basic AI-supported solutions to real-world business situations

👥 Target Audience:

  • Non-technical professionals who need to understand how AI can support decision-making, improve efficiency, and enhance customer-related processes in their daily work

🛠️ Tools & Approach:

  • Articulate Rise
  • Interactions: scenario-based quizzes, flashcards, accordions, scenarios
